Ko sam ja

From the darkest depths of this silly world, i Mirus II, was summoned together with my altruistic and humourous living alter-ego, brother-homie Vlad (Vladius III) from a universe forever enveloped in fog. We primarily came with no goals whatsoever, but steadily grew ambitious and more self-confident. Our family is but a simple one with many problems whom i still adore imencely. Me and my brother were always interested in animation, video games and music, but it has spread to books and fashion. What i wish for one day is to have the proper outlets to show the world my thoughts and ideas.
